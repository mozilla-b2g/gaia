SIM Security

  • SIM PIN
What is a SIM PIN?
A SIM PIN prevents access to the SIM card's cellular data networks. When it's on, any device containing the SIM card will request the PIN upon restart.
A SIM PIN is not the same as the Passcode used to unlock the device.
